Configuration framework · MIT · v0.1

Configure your AI
digital partner in 25 minutes.

You spend 25 minutes in conversation with your AI tool. It produces files on your disk — a Charter, a Schedule, a set of Skills. The OS scheduler runs them. The output lands in your existing Feishu, Notion, or IM. The agent is yours.

# 30 seconds, in any terminal
$ curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Frank-Pu/commando/main/install.sh | sh
$ commando onboard # 25-min dialog → ./my-agent/
$ commando go-live # connect IM + install schedule

What it does

Configuration is what you own. We make it shareable.

Three primitives. No SaaS lock-in. Your partner runs on whatever AI tool you already use.

01 — Charter

The constitution

Your partner's identity, voice, ICP, and red lines. Auto-injected into every call. A markdown file in your repo.

02 — Skills

The discrete tasks

Write a post, debrief a meeting, scan competitors. Activated by Schedule or asked via IM. Each Skill is one SKILL.md.

03 — Schedule

When things fire

Translated into launchd / systemd. The OS runs your partner, not us.

Three real configurations

What does a digital partner actually look like?

Below are three real Charters showing the range. Each is a few hundred lines of markdown that defines the agent.

Role · Growth partner

For the indie product creator

Live for 6+ months serving LeMingle — the canonical reference Configuration in this repo.

"I am [Product]'s growth partner. Voice: plain, data-first, never marketing-speak.
Red lines: AIGC manual-edit ≥ 20%, zero outbound links on Xiaohongshu, marketing density ≤ 20%."
morning_sense 08:00 daily  ·  xhs_draft 11:00 Tue+Fri  ·  weekly_data_review 16:00 Fri  ·  weekly_reflection 19:00 Sun

Role · Personal finance advisor

Always-on watcher of your accounts

Illustrates how strong red lines make the partner safer than a generic assistant.

"I am your personal finance advisor. Red lines: I never execute trades; I never predict markets;
I never recommend high-risk products; my quarterly reports must include a section 'where I might be wrong'."
daily_portfolio_health 07:00  ·  monthly_tax_check 1st 09:00  ·  quarterly_rebalance_review end-of-quarter

Role · Job search coach

3-month sprint partner for switching roles

Time-bounded relationships, with explicit failure criteria built into the Charter.

"I am your career-pivot coach. Voice: warm but no-water. Red lines: I won't pad your résumé;
I won't help you dodge real weaknesses; if 3 months pass with no progress I will say so."
daily_listing_scan 09:00  ·  interview_debrief manual  ·  weekly_pacing_check 19:00 Sun

Get started

Your partner runs tomorrow morning.

Install, onboard, go-live. Tomorrow at 8am, your partner fires its first task and pings you on IM.
